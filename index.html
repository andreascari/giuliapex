Giulia Pex is an illustrator based in Milan, Italy. Born and raised in the faraway country, she started express herself better with images rather than words since she was a kid.

Fallen in love with her camera, she attended the Italian Institute of Photography where she experimented the commingling between her images and drawings. Not really satisfied, she decided to join the illustration class at Scuola del Fumetto to focus on research and improvement of her personal style.

She currently works as a freelancer to save money to afford a personal dog to pet.

If you want to get in touch, drop me an email at giuliapex@gmail.com

Selected Clients: Il Corriere della Sera, Hoppìpolla Edizioni, Presque Lune, FCB Global, Scientific American Mind, Braga+Federico, Franco Angeli Edizioni, Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore.
